We would like to make sure you are fully aware of your Personal Data protection rights. Each User is entitled to the following:

  • The right for any inaccuracies to be corrected (rectification). If any data that we hold about you is incomplete or inaccurate, you are able to require us to correct it;
  • The right to have information deleted (erasure). If you would like us to stop processing your data, you have the right to ask us to delete it from our systems where you believe there is no reason for us to continue processing it;
  • The right to restrict the processing of the data. You may obtain restriction of processing in case the accuracy of Personal Data is contested, or the Processing is unlawful, or you require Personal Data for establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims, and the Data Controller no longer needs it, or the legitimate grounds of the Data Controller are overridden by your objections.

  • The right to portability. You may transfer the data that we hold on you for your own purposes;
  • The right to object to the inclusion of any information. You have the right to object to the way we use your data where we are using it for our legitimate interests;
  • The right to regulate any automated decision-making and profiling of personal data. You have a right not to be subject to automated decision making in way that adversely affects your legal rights.
