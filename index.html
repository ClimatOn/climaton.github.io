SO YOU WANNA MANAGE YOUR CLIMATE?!

ClimatOn: Everything you need to know!

One touch and you make your air pure, its temperature and humidity comfortable, your water hot.

Powerfull cloud servers by AWS

The ClimatOn app is powered by Amazon Web Services so it works always and everywhere. But actually you can manage your climate locally. If your phone is connected to the same Wi-Fi spot as your devices ClimatOn can send commands and get responses even without internet connection. It's a kind of Magic!
